# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 4, 2026

## Overview

Anti Refresh X is a browser extension designed to improve your browsing experience by preventing unwanted page refresh behavior.

We respect your privacy. This extension is built to work locally in your browser and does not collect personal data.

## Information We Collect

Anti Refresh X does **not** collect, store, or sell personal information.

Specifically, we do not collect:

- Name, email, or contact information
- Browsing history for analytics or profiling
- IP address or device identifiers for tracking
- Payment or financial information

## How the Extension Works

The extension runs locally on your device. Its functionality is applied directly in the webpages where it is enabled.

No user content is transmitted to external servers by Anti Refresh X.

## Permissions

Browser permissions requested by the extension are used only to provide core functionality.

Anti Refresh X does not use permissions to:

- Track users across websites
- Build user profiles
- Sell or share user data with third parties

## Third-Party Services

Anti Refresh X does not rely on third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or remote data collection services.

## Data Sharing

We do not share user data with third parties because we do not collect user data.

## Children's Privacy

Anti Refresh X is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. If changes are made, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised.
